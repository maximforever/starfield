Get ready to shoot up flesh-eating space squares! They have stumbled into your corner of the cosmos, and they're not leaving without a fight. Every enemy that makes it past your guard will feast on your health, and watch out - among their ranks are vicious Queen squares ready to take a chomp out of your spacesuit.
SPACE or Left Click to shoot
P to pause the game
A or ' to use berserk
S or ; to use hyperspeed
Fortunately, you'll have some help from floating space debris along the way. Some power-ups are applied instantly (extra bullets and HP), but others (hyperspeed, shield, and berserk) are stored for whenever you're ready to use them. Hover over power-ups to pick them up.

Extra bullets: your spacesuit holds up to 20 total

Berserk: your bullets deal double damage (15 sec)

HP: just floatin' out there in space

Hyperspeed: you go fast, everything else goes slow (15 sec)

Shield: prevents any damage (10 sec)
